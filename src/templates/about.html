About

I'm a frontend engineer with 8 years of experience, currently on a journey to rebuild my fundamentals.

After years of relying on frameworks and abstractions, I decided to go back to basics: hand-written HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript/TypeScript. This site is part of that journey.

Why rebuild fundamentals?

Frameworks are powerful, but they can obscure the underlying technologies. By stripping away abstractions, I'm rediscovering the foundations that make the web work.

What I'm working on

  • Deepening my CSS knowledge (no Tailwind crutch)
  • Building accessible, semantic HTML
  • Understanding browser APIs at a deeper level
  • Writing cleaner TypeScript
